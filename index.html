Dr. Jane Goodall: A Legacy of Compassion

Dr. Jane Goodall interacting with chimpanzees in Gombe National Park.

Dr. Jane Goodall is a world-renowned ethologist and conservationist, known for her groundbreaking work with chimpanzees in Tanzania. Her research transformed the understanding of primates and emphasized the importance of protecting their natural habitats. Beyond her scientific achievements, she founded the Jane Goodall Institute and Roots & Shoots program, advocating for conservation, animal welfare, and humanitarian causes globally.
